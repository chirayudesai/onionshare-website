OnionShare Mobile is Ready for Testing!

The OnionShare teams working with Tor Project, Calyx Institute and Guardian Project, have created the official OnionShare mobile apps for Android and iOS. The apps are now in public beta testing phase, ready for feedback on stability, usability and privacy controls.



WARNING: These apps are not yet audited, and in early alpha/beta state. Please only use for testing.

Features and Functionality

Your Private Productivity Suite.. In Your Pocket!

Share files and host websites right from your phone, all over Tor Onion Services, built by the developers who created Orbot, Briar, Onion Browser and CalyxOS!

Share Files

Ever wanted to share something without also sharing it with Google or Dropbox? Tax documents, medical information, or unpublished research? Share files directly and securely without the middlemen.

OnionService On-Demand

Host a static website and make it accessible through the Tor Browser or Onion Browser without worrying about setting up a server.

OnionShare was developed by Micah Lee, et al. and is open source, released under the GPLv3.
